About Trident Academy

Excellence in Technical Education Since 2000

Our Legacy

Trident Academy of Technology, established in 2000, has been at the forefront of technical education in Eastern India. With over two decades of excellence, we have consistently produced industry-ready professionals who lead technological innovation across the globe.

Our commitment to academic excellence, combined with state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, creates an environment where students thrive and achieve their full potential.
