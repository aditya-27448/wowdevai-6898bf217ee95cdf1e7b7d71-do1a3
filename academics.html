Departments

Explore our specialized departments

Computer Science & Engineering

The CSE department focuses on cutting-edge technologies including AI/ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Software Development. Our curriculum is designed to meet industry demands with hands-on projects and internships.

Key Subjects:

  • • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • • Machine Learning
  • • Database Management
  • • Computer Networks

Facilities:

  • • Advanced Computing Labs
  • • AI/ML Research Center
  • • Software Development Studio
  • • Innovation Lab

Electronics & Communication Engineering

ECE department covers embedded systems, VLSI design, communication systems, and IoT. Students work on real-world projects in telecommunications, robotics, and electronic circuit design.

Key Subjects:

  • • Digital Signal Processing
  • • VLSI Design
  • • Communication Systems
  • • Embedded Systems

Facilities:

  • • Electronics Design Lab
  • • Communication Lab
  • • Microprocessor Lab
  • • VLSI Design Center

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering department emphasizes design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, and automation. Students gain practical experience through workshops, industrial training, and capstone projects.

Key Subjects:

  • • Thermodynamics
  • • Manufacturing Technology
  • • Machine Design
  • • Fluid Mechanics

Facilities:

  • • Manufacturing Workshop
  • • CAD/CAM Lab
  • • Materials Testing Lab
  • • Thermal Engineering Lab
